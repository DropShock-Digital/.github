## Engineering philosophy

- **Source fidelity over vibe-coded polish.** Every pixel and every claim is grounded in real requirements, real copy, and real data.
- **Cost is architecture.** Model routing, context-window management, and infrastructure choices are engineering decisions, not afterthoughts.
- **Agents need boundaries.** Autonomous systems ship with scope, permissions, rollback paths, and human approval at the right moments.
- **Evidence beats narration.** Logs, probes, diffs, screenshots, and test output come before confident claims.
- **Systems survive contact with the real world.** Rate limits, messy inputs, cost ceilings, failing jobs, and skeptical humans — these are design constraints, not excuses.
